About Us

NGALAMANGE GLOBAL LOGISTICS LIMITED is a private logistics and financial services company registered under the Companies Act No. 12 of 2002 in Tanzania. The company is committed to providing comprehensive logistics solutions and diversified financial services.

Company and Transport

Our core services include freight transportation by road, rail, air, and sea, along with pipeline transport. We also offer warehousing, cargo handling, courier activities, and other transportation support services.

In addition to logistics, we provide leasing services for vehicles and machinery, travel agency solutions, and reservation management. The company is also authorized to engage in money management activities, including dealing in securities, foreign currency exchange, lending, and financial investments.

NGALAMANGE GLOBAL LOGISTICS LIMITED is structured as a private company limited by shares. The company is managed by a team of directors, currently led by Bikolimana Joseph Gamuye and Janeth Paul Bahinda.

Our vision is to become a leading provider of logistics and financial solutions in Tanzania and beyond, delivering professional services that drive growth and efficiency for our clients.

NGALAMANGE GLOBAL LOGISTICS LIMITED

NGALAMANGE GLOBAL LOGISTICS LIMITED is a private company registered under the Companies Act No. 12 of 2002 in Tanzania. The company provides a diverse range of logistics and financial services with a commitment to operational excellence and customer satisfaction.

The legal status of the company is Private Company Limited by Shares. The registered office is located in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. The company was incorporated in 2024. The board of directors includes Bikolimana Joseph Gamuye and Janeth Paul Bahinda. Core business activities include freight transport by road, rail, air, sea, and pipeline; warehousing and cargo handling; leasing of motor vehicles and machinery; travel agency and reservation services; and financial services including securities trading, lending, and investment management. NGALAMANGE GLOBAL LOGISTICS LIMITED strives to become a leader in logistics and financial solutions across Tanzania and beyond.

NGALAMANGE GLOBAL LOGISTICS LIMITED is committed to providing customer-centric services with a focus on reliability, transparency, and efficiency. The company works closely with clients to understand their logistics and financial needs, delivering tailored solutions that drive growth and success.

We leverage modern technology to streamline logistics operations, track shipments in real time, and manage financial transactions securely. Our innovative approach ensures timely deliveries and financial services that meet global standards.

With a strategic plan for regional growth, the company aims to expand its services beyond Tanzania, establishing partnerships across East Africa and beyond, to become a trusted logistics and financial service provider throughout the continent.
