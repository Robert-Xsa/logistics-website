Company Overview

NGALAMANGE GLOBAL LOGISTICS LIMITED is a private company registered under the Companies Act No. 12 of 2002 in Tanzania. The company provides a diverse range of logistics and financial services with a commitment to operational excellence and customer satisfaction. The legal status of the company is Private Company Limited by Shares. The registered office is located in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. The company was incorporated in 2024 with a share capital of TZS 20,000,000, divided into 100 shares of TZS 200,000 each, and is limited to 50 members. The board of directors includes Bikolimana Joseph Gamuye and Janeth Paul Bahinda. Core business activities include freight transport by road, rail, air, sea, and pipeline; warehousing and cargo handling; leasing of motor vehicles and machinery; travel agency and reservation services; and financial services including securities trading, forex exchange, lending, and investment management. NGALAMANGE GLOBAL LOGISTICS LIMITED strives to become a leader in logistics and financial solutions across Tanzania and beyond.
